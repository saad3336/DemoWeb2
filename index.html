Our Story

A Quarter Century of Shaping Young Minds

Since 1998, Al-Noor Academy has stood as Rawalpindi's most trusted institution — blending rigorous academics with the timeless values of Islam to produce students who excel in both worlds.

FBISE & Punjab Board Affiliated

Fully recognised. Our Matric students consistently achieve top positions in district results.

Islamic Tarbiyat at the Core

Daily Quran, Seerat curriculum, and a school culture built on adab, discipline, and service.

B.Ed / M.Ed Qualified Faculty

Every teacher is degree-qualified with 5+ years of classroom experience and ongoing training.
